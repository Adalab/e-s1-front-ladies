To front and beyond: Inspired by the girls’ movement “Girls to the front”.

TEAM

What brought us together was our motivation to be part of the world of technology. We dream of being that generation that will reduce the gender gap because we believe that our motivation and effort will be the main fuel to develop our capabilities.

It is not just learning how to code, but to start developing our skills as a team and become successful women in this new IT environment.

Strengths

We are a team that each member brings something unique to the table. Our most powerful weapon is using our different backgrounds and experience to achieve our common goal. Art, science, philosophy, education. That singular perspective provides us a fresh and new point of view on the task that we need to complete.

Weaknesses

Despite the fact that we don't have that much experience on programming, we do have a strong drive for success and willingness to overcome any adversity that we may in counter.
